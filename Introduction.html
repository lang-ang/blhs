Synopsis

Midnight, the hidden time between the day and the day. It's called "Shadow Time." At this untrodden moment, the streets are surrounded by an eerie silence, enough to make those who stray into them shudder. This time wandering the streets, is known as "Shadow", a very strange monster.
On one full moon night, after midnight, the "Shadows" suddenly attack the protagonist. And when the protagonist is cornered, suddenly, the power hidden in his heart is stimulated...
