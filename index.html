"Memento Mori"

It is the fourth proper sequel to the Atlus-developed JRPG series Persona, and the first entry in the so-called New Persona series, released for the PlayStation 2 on July 13, 2006.

Numerous sequels and spin-offs

Persona 3 FES (ペルソナ3 FES, P3F) was released for the PlayStation 2 on April 19, 2007.
Persona 3 Portable (ペルソナ3 ポータブル, P3P) was released for the PlayStation Portable on November 1, 2009. The remake was released worldwide on January 19, 2023, in simplified Chinese for Microsoft Windows, Xbox One, Xbox Series X/S, PlayStation 4, and Nintendo Switch. Launch with Xbox/PC Game Pass. The remake adds one-week change difficulty and mid-game save features that were not available in the original PS2/PSP version.
Persona 3 Reload (Japanese: ペルソナ3 リロード, English: Persona 3 Reload will be available on Microsoft Windows (Microsoft Store, Steam), Xbox Series X/S, Xbox One, PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4 platforms in early 2024. Join the Xbox/PC Game Pass library at launch.
There are also TV animation, theater version, music game "Goddess Chronicles March Night Hot Dance" (P3D) and other derivative works
